01 · RECOMMENDATION SYSTEMSABOUT

Amazon products, ranked.

Building a recommendation system to recommend the best Amazon products to users

Rank & Similarity & Rank Recommendation SystemsMatrix Factorization Recommendation Systems

Model Journey & Outcome

  1. Baseline: Popularity + simple user–user cosine similarity to set a top-N benchmark.
  2. Phase 1: Tuned k-NN (user & item) with normalization and minimum overlap — modest precision uplift.
  3. Phase 2: Matrix factorization (SVD) with tuned factors/regularization — best overall Precision@10/Recall@10 and lowest RMSE.
  4. Final recommendation: Ship tuned SVD for ranking; refresh factors weekly and recs daily; use popularity/new-arrival fallback for cold-start; monitor Precision@10/Recall@10 and RMSE.

02 · Course: Supervised Machine Learning

Prediction system for potential customers

Classifies leads most likely to convert using logistic regression and tree-based models; includes feature engineering and evaluation.

Logistic RegressionRandom ForestsModel EvaluationFeature Engineering

Model Journey & Outcome

  1. Baseline: Majority‑class & simple EDA rules to quantify uplift.
  2. Phase 1: Pruned Decision Tree — reduced overfitting and improved interpretability.
  3. Phase 2: Tuned Random Forest — grid search over `n_estimators`, depth, and split/leaf sizes.
  4. Final recommendation: Deploy the Random Forest as the primary conversion scorer; score daily, route the top decile to Sales, tune threshold to precision/recall targets, and monitor F1; maintain a nurture track for low‑propensity leads.
